About Us


BJ Technology is a startup project of the Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ASOIU). The company was established in 2021 in Baku/Azerbaijan. Our team consists of professionals, who have worked with HPLC for many years and have skills in a wide range of Liquid Chromatography (LC) areas like HPLC, SFC as well as SMB. We are dedicated to our mission to develop phenomenal packing materials and columns for analytical, semi-preparative, preparative, flash, low pressure and gravitation mode LC separations. Our LC phases have a unique combination of high pore volume and surface area, which helps to achieve high mechanical and chemical stability in comparison to other silica packing materials in the market. Our goal is to optimize the chromatographic processes, which will help to make the world green again.
